HOW SHREK JUICES
BECAME SHREK JUICES



Juicing in Pakistan has been taken to the next level. Driven by the lack of healthy and fresh juice bars enjoyed by a variety of taste buds, satisfying conscious consumers from all walks of life, and meeting international hygiene standards, Shrek juices is the brainchild of creating a more refreshing recreational activity for you. Taking in the concept of a local juice shop, we’ve created a juice bar directed towards your love for healthy and freshly squeezed drinks. The need to inculcate health awareness and benefits of juicing is a task that must be fulfilled with passion and sincerity – and we do just that.

The founders of Shrek Juices found their key strength in the concept of belonging to the consumer strata: they are aware of the needs in taste, affordability and hygiene. In fact, the entire way we conduct our business has been developed around the customers to ensure that we are giving them just what they want, not what we think they want. Vigorous focus group research and gaining insight into the requirements of the market helped us in achieving our goal.



WE PUT OUT HEALTHY MISSION WHERE OUR STRAWS ARE.


Our juices, smoothies and bowls are made with high quality, good-for-you ingredients, supplying 250 million servings of fruits and vegetables a year. That's over 249,999,990 more than the daily recommendation.
