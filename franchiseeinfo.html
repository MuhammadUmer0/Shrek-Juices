A Healthy Franchise Opportunity



    We're all about healthy products...healthy franchised businesses too!

Shrek's mission is to be the leading healthy lifestyle brand offering consumers great-tasting and better-for-you products. We believe our commitment to healthy living extends not only to our customers, but also to our franchisees.


Brand recognition - Shrek Juice is the category leader with over 850 units nationwide and unmatched brand recognition. The Shrek brand stands for fun, healthy, on-the-go products in an energizing environment.



Support - Franchising with us means you'll have access to our extensive support structure:


  • Vendors - We source and contract globally to ensure we get the right ingredients delivered to your door at the right prices.
  • Training - We train you to manage a Shrek Juice unit, and we'll train your team to help ensure a successful opening.
  • Operations - Your Franchise Business Consultant will visit you regularly to ensure we are meeting your needs on an ongoing basis.

Shrek Juice is currently seeking qualified franchisees who are as passionate about the Shrek brand as we are. If you would like to learn more, then simply fill out our US Request For Information or our International Inquiries.
