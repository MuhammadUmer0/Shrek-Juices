About Us

Shrek Juice started out as a little juice shop with a big idea - that healthy living starts locally. We've made it our purpose to infuse that thought into everything we do.



What makes House of Juice unique is that we provide both on-premises and off-premises catering. Our home kitchen is located in our juicery & brewery headquarters. We do all our prep and juicing right here in Brooklyn. However, Smoothie Bars, Smoothie Bowl and Food Station menu events are made to order at your event location. Otherwise for off-premises caterings we juice, brew cook the food and bring it to your site. We have served many different venues throughout the greater New York City area as well as out of state events in Texas, Georgia and North Carolina.
